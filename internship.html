Virat Kohli

INDIAN CRICKET CAPTAIN

About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who has represented the Indian national team as a captain in all three formats. Known for his aggressive style and consistent batting performances, Kohli is considered one of the best batsmen in the world.

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli made his international debut in August 2008. He has achieved numerous records and accolades throughout his career.

Notable Achievements

  • ICC Cricketer of the Year: 2017, 2018
  • Padma Shri: 2017
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna: 2018
  • Fastest player to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals
